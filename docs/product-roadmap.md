# Product Roadmap

## Current Focus

Build a responsive web app for calisthenics-first workout logging, progression tracking, and deterministic coaching recommendations.

## MVP Outcomes

- Athletes can create an account and keep their own training data private.
- Athletes can log structured bodyweight workouts quickly on phone-sized screens.
- Leverly can track exercise progressions, readiness, pain, form quality, assistance, load, holds, reps, tempo, and range of motion.
- Recommendations are deterministic, explainable, conservative when safety signals are poor, and editable by the athlete.

## Later Expansion

- Optional AI explanations, summaries, and semantic search.
- Offline-first training workflows and installable PWA behavior.
- Weighted calisthenics, resistance bands, richer analytics, and optional notifications.
